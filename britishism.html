Britishism

What is it?

Britishism is a British slang dictionary. The items in Britishism are sorted by order of appearance.

The words

Faff
 Faffy
Being inefficient.
For example: My brother faffs a lot. He always forgets things.

Mingin
Gross, Disgusting.
For example: Yesterday, I ate soup and it was mingin! I couldn't even eat it!

Cuppa
Short for "Cup of tea".
For example: Every winter day, when I get home, I drink a cuppa and eat cookies.

Buttie
Sarnie
A sandwich.
For example: Every morning, I make myself a buttie with cheese and avocado.
