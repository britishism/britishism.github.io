Britishism

What is it?

Britishism is a British to English dictionary. The items in Britishism are sorted by order of appearance in the lessons.

The words

Faff
 Faffy
Being inefficient.
For example: My brother faffs a lot. He always forgets things.
Mingin
Gross, Disgusting.
For example: Yesterday, I ate soup and it was mingin! I couldn't even eat it!
Cuppa
Short for "Cup of tea".
For example: Every winter day, when I get home, I drink a cuppa and eat cookies.
Buttie
Sarnie
A sandwich.
For example: Every morning, I make myself a buttie with cheese and avocado.
Spend a penny
Go to the bathroom/toilet.
For example: When I eat something bad, I have to spend a penny, before I vomit it.
Myther
To annoy/bother.
For example: My sister always mythers me.
Used only at Manchester.
